The Identity Manager

The Identity Manager is the browser or central control that facilitates the creation and management of the identities used to sign into all æpps. This is the frame of the entire aeternity user experience serving as digital home base.

Manages Identities

Identity Manger is your first ability to participate in and contribute to our blockchain ecosystem. You can easily create as many identities as you wish based on your activity preferences.

Activates Currency

This core app allows you to use tokens in real time. Forming the basis for the only non-theoretical way to utilize this currency, your key identifies your transactions and keeps a transaction history on the chain.
